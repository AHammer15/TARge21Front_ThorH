Scrambled eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingridients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in frying pan at medium heat
  2. Gently mix eggs and cream in bowl
  3. Once nutter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. using spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds
